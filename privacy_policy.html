Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 15, 2025

Developer: Abdul Manan

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, TMG (the “App”), available on the Google Play Store.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information:
    • Full name
    • Email address
    • Password (securely encrypted and not accessible to us directly)
  • Attendance and Availability Data:
    • Selected available dates for the current and next month
    • Attendance records marked within the app
  • Location Data:
    • The app collects your device’s latitude and longitude when you mark attendance. This is only done when you actively submit your attendance for verification purposes.
  • Admin Access:
    • Admin accounts have access to employee attendance data, which may be exported as Excel files for administrative use only.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To register and authenticate users
  • To store and manage employee availability and attendance
  • To verify attendance locations (via GPS coordinates)
  • To allow administrators to monitor and export attendance records

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • With system administrators for attendance tracking and reporting
  • As required by law or legal process

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption of sensitive data and restricted access to attendance records.

5. Location Data Usage

Location data is used only at the moment of marking attendance, and solely for the purpose of verifying that the user is at a valid location. It is not tracked continuously.

6. Data Retention

We retain user and attendance data as long as your account remains active or as needed to fulfill administrative and legal purposes.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for use by employees and administrators and is not designed for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage users to review it regularly. Any significant changes will be communicated through the app.
